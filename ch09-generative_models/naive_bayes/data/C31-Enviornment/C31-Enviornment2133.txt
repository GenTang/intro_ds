河流水质预测的因素状态网络模型研究
郭宗楼　 马帆　 
摘　要：建立了一种河流水质预测的因素状态人工神经网络模型，其算例的计算结果表明，这一种方法有着极大的研究意义和广阔的应用前景。
关键词：水质预测；　因素状态；　人工神经网络
分类号：X32　文献标识码：A

Study on Factor-State Artificial Neural Network Model for Prediction of Water Quality in a River
GUO Zong-lou（Department of Agriculture Engineering,Zhejiang University,Hangzhou　310029，China）　
MA Fan（Department of Agriculture Engineering,Zhejiang University,Hangzhou　310029，China）　
Abstract：A factor-state artificial neutral network model for prediction of water quality in a river has been constructed.The results of a case study show that the model is an excellent one and very important to study,and this has very good future on its application.
Keywords：water quality prediction,　factor-state;　artificial neutral network
