绿藻对硝基苯类化合物的富集与释放研究
陆光华　 赵元慧　 汤洁　 
摘　要：测定了硝基苯、2，4-二硝基甲苯、2，6-二硝基甲苯及硝基氯苯(邻、间、对)在绿藻体内富集及释放趋势。其生物富集因子由小到大的顺序为：硝基苯<2，6-二硝基甲苯<2，4-二硝基甲苯<硝基氯苯(邻、间、对)。富集与释放曲线表明，所有化合物在实验开始的0.5～1h即达到最大富集量，2h后基本达到稳态。硝基苯、2，4-二硝基甲苯及2，6-二硝基甲苯的释放速率很快，0.5～1h达到最大释放量。6种有机物的生物富集因子与辛醇/水分配系数相关很好，生物富集过程符合脂水平衡分配模型。
关键词：硝基苯类化合物；　生物富集；绿藻；分配过程

Bioconcentration and Elimination of Nitrobenzenes by the Algae (Scenedesmus obliquus)
LU Guang-hua（Department of Environmental Science,Northeast Normal University, Changchun 130024, China）　
ZHAO Yuan-hui（Department of Environmental Science,Northeast Normal University, Changchun 130024, China）　
TANG Jie（Environment and Construction College, Jilin University, Changchun 130021, China）　

Abstract：Bioconcentration potentials were determined for nitrobenzene,2,4-and 2,6-dinitrotoluene,and 3 nitrochlorobenzenes (ortho,meta and para) using the algae (S.obliquus).The order of their bioconcentration factors (BCF)was: NB<2.6-DNT<2,4-DNT<NClE.The sorption curves showed that sorption was up to the greatest degree within 1 h and followed by little change from 2 h to 10 h.The desorption of NB,2,4-DNT and 2,6-DNT was rapid and reached the greatest degree at 0.5～1 h.A strong linear correlation was evident between BCF and the octanol/water partition coefficient (KOW ),and bioconcentration conforms to the model of partition between lipid and water.
Keywords：nitrobenzenes; bioconcentration; algae; partition
