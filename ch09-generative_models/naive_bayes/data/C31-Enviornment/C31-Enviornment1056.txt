水环境质量评价的普适指数公式
李祚泳　 郭丽婷　 欧阳洁　 
摘　要：在适当设定水质指标的“基准”值情况下，用相对于“基准”值的指标相对值替换对数形式的指数公式中的水质指标值，采用遗传算法优化不同指标的指数公式中的参数，得到对多项指标通用的水质普适指数公式；并用折衷型激励功效函数将单项指标的指数加权为综合指数。公式的正确性已被多处水质评价检验。
关键词：水质评价；　指数；　遗传算法；　参数优化；　功效函数

An Universal Index Formula Suited to Water Quality Evaluation
LI Zuo-yong（Chengdu University of Information Technology, Chengdu 610041, China）　
GUO Li-ting（Chengdu University of Information Technology, Chengdu 610041, China）　
OUYANG Jie（Guangdong University of Technology, Guangzhou 510090, China）　

Abstract：Based on hypothesis of “base value” of water quality parameter,the monitoring values of parameters in the water quality index formula with logarithm function form were replaced by their relative values,and the optimum for coefficients of different indexes in formula was carried out using genetic algorithms,and then an universal index formula of water quality suited to multi-parameter was presented.The comprehensive index of water quality can be calculated in the aid of the compromise active function weighting for each index.The formula was verified by assessing the water quality states of many spots with the above method.
Keywords：water quality evaluation;　index;　genetic algorithms;　parameter optimum;　active function
