卫星的防污染技术
舒伟民　 
摘　要：在分析了国内外光学遥感卫星红外通道被水汽污染而失效的教训基础上，提出了光学遥感卫星必需全过程（从卫星设计、研制、发射至在轨运行）实施严格的防污染控制的设计思想和理论。系统地介绍了FY-1C星的防污染技术特点和经验。在轨运行表明，FY-1C所采取的一系列防污染措施是正确的。
关键词：气象卫星；防污染技术；环境条件
分类号：V474.2+4　文献标识码：A
文章编号：1006－1630(2001)02－0069－05
Anti-Contamination Technology for Satellite
SHU Wei-min（No.509 Institute of SAST, Shanghai 200240, China）　

Abstract：Anti-pollution is a key technology for optical remote sensing satellite. In th e paper, the cause of failure for infrared channel of optical remote satellite a t home and abroad is analyzed in an all round way. The Anti-pollution technolog y for FY-1C is systematically summarized. It is the first time in China to considere this technology on remote sensing satellite during the total process. The successful operation of FY-1C shows that the key problem has been solved.
Keywords：Meteorological Satellite；Anti-pollution technology; Environmental condition
作者简介：舒伟民(1943-)，男，高级工程师，FY-1卫星遥感系统副主任设计师，研究领域：卫星遥感。
